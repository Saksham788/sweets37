About Us

Established in 1940, our sweet shop holds a legacy of over 80 years. The foundation was laid by the father of our grandfather, but the true credit for making our shop a beloved and trusted name in the region goes to Munna Lal Ji Gupta, whose dedication, honesty, and respectful service turned a humble sweet shop into a brand.

We take pride in crafting purely handmade sweets, following traditional methods passed down through generations.
